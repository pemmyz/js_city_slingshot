Controls & Instructions

Objective: Knock down all the buildings!

  • Launch: Click and drag from the brown sling on the left. Release to fire.
  • Reset Scene (R): Resets the simulation, applying any new building settings from the sliders.
  • Toggle Air Drag (D): Toggles air resistance on the projectile.
  • Toggle Destructible Mode (H): Toggles whether blocks take damage and break.
  • Help Menu (H): Toggles this help window.

Building Controls

Use the sliders in the top-left panel to customize the city generation. After changing the values, press the R key to generate a new city with those parameters.
